<H1>USA Supermarket Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)</H1>
This repository contains the exploratory data analysis (EDA) of a USA supermarket dataset. 

The goal of this project is to extract meaningful insights from the data 
1. To understand Sales Patterns,
2. Customer Behavior,
3. Product Performance,
4. And other Important Metrics.
<br>
<H3>Project Overview</H3>
The dataset used in this project includes information on:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Sales transactions</li>
<li>Product Details</li>
<li>Customer Demographics</li>
<li>Order and Shipping Details</li>
</ul>
<br>
The Analysis covers various aspects, including Data Cleaning, Data Visualization, and Pattern Identification.
<br>

The dataset contains 9,426 records and 24 columns, with fields such as:<br>
<b>Order ID, Customer ID, Product Category, Unit Price, Sales, Order Date, Shipping Mode, Profit, And many others.</b>
<br>
<br>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
The main objectives of the analysis are:
<ul>
<li>To explore and clean the dataset.</li>
<li>To analyze the sales patterns and identify trends.</li>
<li>To examine customer behavior based on demographics.</li>
<li>To explore product performance by category.</li>
<li>To explore product performance by category.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<h3>Analysis</h3>
The EDA is broken down into the following sections:

<ul>
<li>Data Cleaning: Checking for missing values and correcting inconsistencies.</li>
<li>Univariate Analysis: Exploring individual variables like Order Priority, Shipping Mode, Customer Segment, etc.</li>
<li>Bivariate Analysis: Examining the relationships between variables, such as Product Category and Profit.</li>
<li>Time Series Analysis: Analyzing sales trends across different years.</li>
<li>Geographical Analysis: Investigating sales distribution across different states.</li>
</ul>

<br>
<h3>Key Insights</h3>
<ol>
<li>Order Priority: The majority of orders fall under 'High' and 'Low' priority categories.</li>
<li>Shipping Modes: Most products are shipped using 'Regular Air', followed by 'Delivery Truck' and 'Express Air'.</li>
<li>Sales Trends: Sales increased significantly in 2013 compared to previous years.</li>
<li>Top 5 States by Sales: California, Texas, Illinois, New York, and Florida.</li>
<li>Profitability: The Technology category generated the highest profits, while the Office Supplies category had the highest Product Base Margin.</li>
<li>Customer Segments: The majority of customers belong to the 'Corporate' and 'Consumer' segments.</li>
</ol>
